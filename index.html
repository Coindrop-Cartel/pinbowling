Welcome to PinBowling

A competitive pinball scoring system that translates high scores into bowling frames.

About the Project

PinBowling maps pinball performance across multiple machines into a standardized 10-frame bowling game. Organizers define scoring thresholds for each machine, and players compete to see who can bowl the perfect 300 game on pinball!

The system automatically calculates strikes, spares, and total scores following standard bowling rules based on the machine difficulty you configure.
